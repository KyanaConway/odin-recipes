Fruit Dip

Fruit Dip for beginners

This simple recipe can be made by anytone. It contains only two ingredients and easy to whip up for any gathering!

Ingredients

  • 2x Marshmellow Dip
  • 1x Philadelphia Cream Cheese Block

Steps

  1. Let both ingredients sit out unrefridgerated. This will make it easier to mix smooth
  2. Completely empty both containers of marshmellow dip into bowl
  3. Put one block of cream cheese into bowl with marshmellow dip
  4. Mix together until you achieve desired smooth consistency!
