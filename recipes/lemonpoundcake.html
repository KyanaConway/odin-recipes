Lemon Pound Cake

Lemon pound cake recipe from scratch

This recipe is more tedious than our Fruit Dip Recipe but even more rewarding!

Ingredients

  • Half a Dozen Eggs
  • 1 Box of Swans Down Cake Flour
  • 2 Blocks of Cream Cheese
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Lemon Extract
  • Lemon Juice
  • Dominos Powered Sugar
  • Dominos Cane sugar

Cake Steps

  1. Let all ingredients sit out unrefridgerated to make the mixing process quicker. This will aid in having a smooth batter!
  2. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees and grab a big bowl
  3. Swift 3 cups of flour into a big bowl
  4. Add in 1 block of cream cheese
  5. Sift in 3 cups of cane sugar
  6. Mix flour and cream cheese together mixer until smooth
  7. Add in eggs. Mix these one by one and not all together. This step is crucial for a fully cooked caked!
  8. Add two table spoons of both vanilla and lemon extract in. Don't add too much vanilla because this extract is very overwhelming in flavor.
  9. After your extract, slowly pour your batter evenly into desired cake shaper and place in oven. I prefer the bundt shape!
  10. Levae in oven for exactly an hour and a half and remove!

Glaze steps

  1. Grab smaller bowl and scoop half a block of cream cheese in
  2. Add Lemon Juice
  3. Add powdered sugar for consistency
  4. The amount for these ingredients are based on prefrence. Experient until you acheive desired flavor and texture!
