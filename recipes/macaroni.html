Macaroni

Ingredients

  • Macaroni noodles
  • 1 block of Nice n Cheesy
  • 1 block of Philadelphia cream cheese
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • Crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Boil you noodles
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  3. Cut up Nice n Cheesy and Philadelphia cream cheese into small cube blocks
  4. Drain noodles
  5. Pour noodles into aluminium
  6. Add you Nice n Cheesy and cream cheese to pan
  7. Pour in evaporated milk
  8. Stir and mix ingredients evenly
  9. Add crushed red pepper
  10. Cover with tin foil and put int oven for 1 hour while watching
  11. Remove and Enjoy!
